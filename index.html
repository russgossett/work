Composer 2

Playlist management and scheduling web app

Watch Demo

NPR Digital Services' most widely used web application, station managers and hosts in public radio login to Composer everyday to setup programs schedules, manage, import and export playlists. The problem: it had not beed redesigned in over 10 years.

My role in re-engineering this web app started by gathering user research and feedback from local stations and continued with designing a series of experience documentation, such as userflows, wireframes and UI prototypes. And as a way to involve stakeholders, our team conducted usability tests with hosts and stations managers along the way. I continued to iterate through a number of design solutions based feedback. The result: a modern design built with cutting-edge technology that creates an adaptive experience stations love.

ABC Shore

Edu-tainment for iOS Kids

Download the App

As the UX/UI lead, I worked with a small team of iOS developers to create this game for children. The goal was to improve alphabet recognition in an engaging way using touch, sounds and visual cues.

In the game, children discover letters in the sand, which they can uncover by digging. Each letter is forms a word and each completed word is a key to unlocking a prize.
